1. Deep Breathing

Take slow, deep breaths. Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 4, exhale for 4. Repeat 5 times.

2. Grounding (5-4-3-2-1)

Focus on 5 things you see, 4 you feel, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste. It brings you to the present moment.

3. Gratitude Journaling

Write down 3 things you're grateful for. It rewires your brain for positivity and peace.

4. 2-Minute Meditation

Close your eyes, focus on your breath. Allow thoughts to come and go without judgment.

5. Light Stretching

Stretch your body gently. It relieves muscle tension and improves blood flow.

6. Nature Walk

Take a 10-minute walk in nature. Notice the sounds, colors, and smells around you.

7. Mindful Tea Break

Make a warm cup of tea. Sip slowly, savoring every sensation and aroma.
